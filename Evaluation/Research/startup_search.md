# Startup Search Evaluation Benchmark

This document defines the evaluation framework for the **Startup Search** capability and includes a completed, high-credibility example task.

---

## 1. Benchmark Specification

### 📥 Input
*   **Target Startup Name:** Name of the early-stage or private technology firm (e.g., `"Perplexity AI"`).
*   **Sector / Founders (Optional):** Contextual parameters (e.g., `"Search Engine"`, `"Aravind Srinivas"`).

### 📤 Expected Output
A structured, cited research report containing:
1.  **Overview:** Startup mission, core product, and launch year.
2.  **Founding Team:** Key personnel, previous affiliations, and executive roles.
3.  **Funding & Valuation:** Series rounds, lead investors, capital raised, and valuation details.
4.  **Core Technology:** AI models, proprietary data pipelines, or software platforms.
5.  **Market Position:** Primary competitors, growth indicators, and user adoption rates.
6.  **Recent News:** New product releases, strategic hiring, or partnerships.
7.  **Sources & Citations:** Explicit mapping of news logs and investor releases.

### ⚖️ Evaluation Criteria
*   **Financial Traceability:** Correct details of valuation milestones and funding rounds (Target: 100% correct).
*   **Organizational Mapping:** Accurate details of co-founders and executive team changes.
*   **Product Analysis:** Deep understanding of the startup's tech stack and differentiation.
