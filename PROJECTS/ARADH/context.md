# ARADH Context

## Mission
Team and Agent Work Allocation Engine.

## Purpose
To optimize the distribution of work across human and AI agents, ensuring balanced load and efficient execution.

## Responsibilities
* **Work Allocation:** Determining who should work on what based on skill and availability.
* **Team Workload Balancing:** Monitoring and adjusting assignments to prevent burnout or bottlenecks.
* **Agent-to-Human Assignment:** Managing the hand-off and collaboration between AI agents and human team members.
* **Execution Ownership:** Tracking the lifecycle of work items from assignment to completion.

## Current Status
* Active: Initial definition and mission hardening.

## Approved Decisions
* ARADH is the primary engine for work allocation.
