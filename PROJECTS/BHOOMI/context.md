# BHOOMI Context

## Mission
Build an Agriculture Intelligence and Farmer Assistance Ecosystem.

## Purpose
To empower farmers with data-driven insights, proactive advisory, and an intelligent multi-agent support system to improve crop yields and economic outcomes.

## Core Modules
* **Disease Detection:** Automated identification of crop pests and diseases.
* **Crop Advisory:** Personalized guidance on planting, irrigation, and fertilization.
* **Weather Intelligence:** Hyper-local weather forecasting and risk mitigation.
* **Market Intelligence:** Real-time pricing, trend analysis, and supply chain insights.
* **Human-in-the-loop Validation:** Expert verification layer for high-impact advisories.
* **Multi-Agent Architecture:** Collaborative AI agents specialized in distinct agricultural domains.

## Current Status
Architecture and agent ecosystem under active development.

## Approved Decisions
* BHOOMI transitions from a resource management focus to a comprehensive Agriculture Intelligence ecosystem.
* Human-in-the-loop validation is mandatory for critical advisory outputs.

## Next Steps
* Finalize agent interaction protocols within the multi-agent architecture.
* Develop initial prototypes for Disease Detection and Crop Advisory modules.
