# NAARTH Context

## Mission
Responsibility Mapping and Task Distribution.

## Purpose
To ensure every task has a clear ownership structure and escalation path, preventing accountability gaps.

## Responsibilities
* **Owner Identification:** Determining who is primary on a task.
* **Reviewer Assignment:** Identifying who validates the work.
* **Approver Designation:** Identifying the final authority for sign-off.
* **Escalation Path:** Defining the sequence of authority if a task stalls or fails.

## Current Status
* Active: Re-hydrating from old RBAC definition to Responsibility Mapping engine.

## Approved Decisions
* NAARTH is NOT an RBAC agent.
* NAARTH does not manage permissions.

## Next Steps
* Integrate with ARADH for workload-aware distribution.
